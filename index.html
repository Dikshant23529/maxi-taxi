Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can a wheelchair taxi actually arrive?

Most pickups across Perth metro land within 20–30 minutes of booking, thanks to our three-partner fleet network covering different zones of the city.

Do you accept NDIS and government subsidy bookings?

Yes — we work with plan-managed, self-managed and agency-managed NDIS plans, and accept government subsidy cards directly in the vehicle for eligible passengers.

How many wheelchairs can one vehicle carry?

Our maxi taxis carry up to 2 wheelchairs, or 1–2 wheelchairs plus 4–5 additional ambulant passengers, depending on the vehicle assigned.

Can you handle a large group booking?

Yes — for parties, events and family trips of 12 people or more, let us know in the notes field and dispatch will arrange the right combination of vehicles.

Do you cover suburbs outside Perth CBD?

Yes — our network covers Perth metro and the surrounding Western Australia suburbs, from Mandurah in the south to Joondalup in the north.

Need a wheelchair taxi right now?

Our dispatch line is open 24 hours a day, every day of the year. Call, WhatsApp, or fill in the form above.
